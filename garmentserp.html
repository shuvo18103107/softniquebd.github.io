Garments ERP Softwar

Garments ERP Softwar

SoftniqueBd is one of the leading Garments ERP solution provider, especially for Garments industries which is integrated with different modules that cover all the aspects of your Garments Business. This Garments ERP System supports multi-currency, multi-Company and multi-location based operations. It aims at keeping track of all the activities including receiving buyer order, costing of order, resource planning, procurement of raw materials, production management, inventory management, import-export process, order reconciliation process etc. It’s also integrated with other modules of Smart ERP including finance, accounts, HRM, supply-chain etc. With this automated solution you can easily track your business activities and entire operations of your garments manufacturing process.

Garments ERP Softwar Feature

Merchandising Management

With the help of this module you can manage Buyer Order, Style Setup, Sample Details with Approval & comments different aspects, Costing, pre-costing assumption, Purchase Order(PO) entry, PO(purchase order) Details, Order Information form, Order confirmation, Yarn booking, Knitting Program, Fabric Dyeing,Accessories booking and so on.

Knitting and Dyeing Program

The Knitting and Dyeing module is a great help for the Garments industry in delivering the product. This module consists of functionalities like Knitting and Dyeing planning, machine scheduling, raw material usage, Knit Yarn Issue, Gray Fabric Receive, Gray Fabric Issue, Finish Fabric Issue Receive.

Inventory Management

Our inventory module can be used to track the stock of items. Items can be identified. Using that unique numbers inventory system can keep track of items and trace their current location in the organization. The inventory module includes functionalities like inventory control, master units, stock utilization reporting, etc.

Production Management

A complete look at the management of an apparel manufacturing system. From upper management functions to employees on the floor, this module also includes sections on information flow, marketing, preseason planning, and much more.

Accounts Financial Management

The main function of this section is Voucher Entry, Voucher Posting or approval. Voucher Entry based on voucher type as Cash Payment, Cash Received, Bank Payment, Bank Received, contra account & Journal. It also compatible with multiple heads (COA) with different Credit(Cash or Bank Head) & Printing or you can also export as extension PDF, doc.

Commercial & System Configuration

Commercial and LC management system for establishing LC in favor of seller or exporter. Smart ERP LC management gives you the guarantee to make all the payments of the particular sum in a specified currency providing the seller with a defined condition and submitting their prescribed documents. Particularly deals with Garments import and export related business.

HR & Payroll Management (HRM)

Human Resource module helps the HR team for efficient management of human resources. HR module helps to manage employee information, track employee records like performance reviews, designations, job descriptions, skill matrix, time & attendance tracking. An anther sub module in the HR module is Payroll System which helps to manage salaries, payment reports, etc.

Attendance Management

HRM Module gives you an easy way to manage the attendance of your employees. The system gives you all the functionalities required to meet your customized attendance management needs. The software takes your input and performs rest of the attendance calculation.

Scheduling & Time-Table Management

HRM Module Using web-enabled and self-service concepts, management of vacation and leave requests was never easier. This integrated module enables online processing of requests and approval for leave and vacation time.
