Point of Sale - POS Software

Point of Sale (POS software) is the system where your customers make payments for goods or services offered by your business. POS transactions can occur directly or online, and receipts are printed or generated electronically. Cloud-based POS systems are becoming increasingly popular among store owners in Bangladesh. SoftniqueBD provides highly secure Point of Sale – POS Software solutions.SoftniqueBD POS software is very easy to use; so, you can manage your business very comfortably. We offer the most competitive POS Software price in Bangladesh with easy setup and management. Therefore, grow your business with the help of our POS System from one store to many.
